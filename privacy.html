Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

RadioFlux ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally

RadioFlux stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Favorites: Your list of favorite radio stations
  • Recent Stations: Stations you've recently listened to
  • App Preferences: Your language, theme, and playback settings
  • EULA Acceptance: Record of your acceptance of our terms

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Payment information

Third-Party Services

RadioFlux uses the following third-party services to provide its functionality:

Radio Browser API

  • Purpose: Search and discover radio stations
  • Data shared: Search queries (station names, country filters)
  • Privacy Policy: radio-browser.info

Remote blacklist (GitHub)

  • Purpose: Download a moderation blacklist used to filter some stations (by UUID and/or URL)
  • Data shared: No user data is sent intentionally; your IP address and basic HTTP metadata may be visible to GitHub when fetching the file
  • Source: raw.githubusercontent.com

LRCLIB

  • Purpose: Retrieve song lyrics
  • Data shared: Artist name and song title (from station metadata)
  • Privacy Policy: lrclib.net

Station logos (images)

  • Purpose: Display station logos when a station provides an image URL
  • Data shared: Your IP address is visible to the image host when the app downloads the logo
  • Note: Each station / image host may have its own privacy practices

Audio Streams

  • Purpose: Play radio content
  • Data shared: Your IP address is visible to radio station servers when streaming
  • Note: Each radio station may have its own privacy practices

Data Security

All data stored by RadioFlux remains on your device and is protected by your device's security measures. We do not have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

RadioFlux does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
  • Portability: Your favorites and settings are stored in standard formats
